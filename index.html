Interaction Design is the design of interactive products, services and experiences.

“Interaction Design is the creation of a dialogue between a person and a product, system, or service. This dialogue is both physical and emotional in nature and is manifested in the interplay between form, function, and technology as experienced over time.” - John Kolko, Author of Thoughts on Interaction Design (2011)
